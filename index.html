Meet Your Coach

Coach Silas

Silas is a Tinman triathlete, endurance coach, and the founder of Kinetic. Based in Nairobi, he has spent years training runners and triathletes — from first-time 5K runners to marathon veterans and Ironman competitors.

He doesn't just hand you a plan and disappear. He structures every session with intent — every tempo run, every interval, every strength circuit has a purpose. He tells you what to do, how to do it, and why it matters.

"I don't want athletes who just run. I want athletes who understand their training. When you know why Tuesday is tempo and Sunday is long and easy — that's when you stop being a jogger and start being a runner."
